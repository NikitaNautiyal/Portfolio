How I Work

Good design is a
method, not luck.

01

Discover

I start with listening — users, stakeholders, data. I map what people say, what they do, and what they actually need. Research shapes everything that follows.

02

Define

From insights, I distill a clear problem statement and design principles. Clarity at this stage saves weeks downstream and eliminates guesswork.

03

Design

Wireframes to high-fidelity. I iterate fast and test with real users before any pixel is finalized. Every decision is grounded in evidence.

04

Deliver

Annotated specs, component libraries, interaction notes. I stay involved through development so the design vision survives contact with code.
